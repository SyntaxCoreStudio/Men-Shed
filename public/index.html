Welcome to Carina Men's Shed

A welcoming space where men can share skills, enjoy company, and work on projects together. Whether you're tinkering, building, or just having a chat, our shed is a place to belong.

Our Goals

The Shed is a place where we come to enjoy each other's company — sometimes working together on a project, or maybe on our own. The main objectives are sharing life's journey, swapping ideas and concerns, and becoming involved in community and personal activities to further enhance our health and well being. There is a wide choice of activities on offer. That's because the Men's Shed movement is based on the belief that men communicate better shoulder to shoulder than face-to-face. It is not only the activities themselves that are important; it's the opportunities they create for our members to get to know each other better. You don't have to be an expert on anything to be in our Shed. That being said, we are self-managing and self-funding so we need to pool our skills to survive and grow as an organization.

How do our member's benefit?

We believe that men can live longer, healthier, and happier lives by:

  • enjoying the camaraderie of other men with similar interests
  • applying our combined knowledge, experience and skills
  • sharing stories
  • mentoring others
  • finding new opportunities and interests
  • stimulating imaginations and offering new challenges
  • rejuvenating spirits; and
  • just having fun

New members are always welcome. The shed is open to men of all ages, backgrounds and abilities. The Shed has an ideal location at the heart of the Clem Jones Centre campus at Carina. So why don't you come along one day (09:00 on Wednesday morning is best) and see what you think. We are certain you will find it worthwhile.
